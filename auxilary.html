Auxiliary Verbs

1. To Be

is/are/was/were...
She is a teacher.
Nuer: Jɛn ɛ ŋi̱i̱c. Amharic: አስተማሪ ነች። āsitemarī nech.
They are happy.
Nuer: kɛn tɛth lo̱ckiɛn Amharic: ደስተኞች ናቸው። desitenyochi nachewi.

2. To Have

We have a good neighbor.
Nuer: te kɔn kɛ guän thieekä mi gɔaa Amharic: ጥሩ ጎረቤት አለን። t’iru gorebēti ālen.
I have a new car.
Nuer: Ɣän ta̱a̱kɛ thurbil mi̱ pay ben. Amharic: አዲስ መኪና አለኝ። ādīs mekīna āleny.

3. To Do

Do you want to go?
Nuer: ji̱n go̱o̱ri ɣöö bi jiɛn? Amharic: መሄድ ትፈልጋለህ? mehēdi tifeligaleh?
I do my homework every day.
Nuer: ɛ Ɣän la̱t cieŋda̱ ɛ lät ni ciaŋ. Amharic: የቤት ስራዬን በየቀኑ እሰራለሁ። yebēt sirayēn beyek’enu iseralehu.

4. Can

mi deri luäŋ
I can speak three languages.
Nuer: Ɣän ruacä kɛ thuk diɔ̱k. Amharic: ሶስት ቋንቋዎችን መናገር እችላለሁ. sosit k’wanik’wawochin menageri ichilalehu.
Can you help me?
Nuer: Ji̱n deri ɣä luäk? Amharic: ልትረዳኝ ትችላለህ፧ litiredany tichilalehi፧.

5. Will

mi bi la̱t nhiam
I will help you with your work.
Nuer: Ɣän bä ji̱ luäk kɛ la̱tdu. Amharic: በስራዎ እረዳዎታለሁ. besirawo iredawotalehu.
We will go to church tomorrow.
Nuer: kɔn banɛ wä duel kuɔth i ruun. Amharic: ነገ ወደ ቤተ ክርስቲያን እንሄዳለን። nege wede bēte kirisitīyani inihēdaleni.

6. Should

mi tho̱pi luek
You should see a doctor.
Nuer: Ji̱n biɛ dhil wä kä di̱ktöör. Amharic: ሐኪም ማየት አለብዎት. ḥākīmi mayeti ālebiwoti.
We should read for the exam.
Nuer: Kɔn banɛ dhil kuen kɛ kui̱ ɣɔ̱nä. Amharic: ለፈተና ማንበብ አለብን. lefetena manibebi ālebini.

7. May

mi diɛwi
You may leave now.
Nuer: Dɔ̱ŋ deri jiɛɛn ɛn täämɛ. Amharic: አሁን ትተው መሄድ ይችላሉ። āhuni titewi mehēdi yichilalu.
May I come in?
Nuer: Ɣän dëë ben rɛc? Amharic: ልግባ? ligiba?

8. Must

mi bi dhil la̱t
You must finish your work.
Nuer: Kä ji̱n biɛ dhil thuk ɛn la̱tdu. Amharic: ስራህን መጨረስ አለብህ። sirahini mech’eresi ālebihi.
We must leave by 5 PM.
Nuer: Bi kɔn dhil jiɛɛn kɛ thaak 5 PM. Amharic: ከቀኑ 5 ሰአት ላይ መውጣት አለብን። kek’enu 5 se’āti layi mewit’ati ālebini.
