Conditional Sentences

1. Zero Conditional

The zero conditional is used for general truths or scientific facts.

jɛn dhɛrö kɔndi̱cinal ɛ mi̱ lät kɛ kui̱ thuɔ̱ɔ̱kni̱ diaal

Formula: If + present simple, + present simple
Example: If you respect your husband, he love you.
Nuer: Mi luthi cɔwdu, jɛn nhɔkɛ ji̱ .| Amharic:ባልሽን የምታከብር ከሆነ ይወድሻል. balishini yemitakebiri kehone yiwedishali
Example: If you heat water to 100°C, it boils.
Nuer: Mi ci pi̱w lëth ɛ wä kä 100°C, bɛ cu puär.| Amharic: ውሃውን እስከ 100 ዲግሪ ሴንቲግሬድ ካሞቁ, ያፈላል.
Example: If you don’t eat, you get hungry.
Nuer: Mi /ciä mi̱thi, lɛ buɔth ji ɛnäk. | Amharic: ካልበላህ ይራባል. kalbelahi yirabali
Example: If I drink too much coffee, I can't sleep at night.
Nuer: Mi cä bun math ɛlɔ̱ŋ, /ci Ɣän ɛ ni̱n kɛ wäär. | Amharic: ቡና አብዝቼ ከጠጣሁ ሌሊት መተኛት አልችልም።

2. First Conditional

The first conditional is used for real and possible situations in the future.

Kä kɔndi̱cinal in nhiam ɛ mi̱ lät kɛ kui̱ kä tin thuɔ̱ɔ̱k kɛnɛ tin de rɔ̱ lot rɛy gua̱th mi̱ bi̱ ben

Formula: If + present simple, + will + verb( v1)
Example: If it rains tomorrow, I will stay home.
Nuer: Mi wä nhiaal dɛ̈m iruun, bä te cieŋ. | Amharic: ነገ ዝናብ ከሆነ ቤት እቆያለሁ. nege zinabi kehone bēti ik’oyalehu
Example: If you study hard, you will pass the exam.
Nuer: Mi ci kuen a bum, bi ɣɔ̱n bäl. | Amharic: ጠንክረህ ከተማርክ ፈተናውን ያልፋል
Example: If they invite me, I will go to the party.
Nuer: Mi cikɛ ɣä cɔl, bä wä ŋarä .| Amharic: ከጋበዙኝ ወደ ግብዣው እሄዳለሁ።

3. Second Conditional

The second conditional is used for hypothetical situations that are unlikely or impossible.

ɛ mi̱ lat kɛ mi /ci̱ de mal tuok kiɛ mi /ci rɔ lot

Formula: If + past simple, + would + base verb
Example: If I were you, I wouldn't mention it.
Nuer: Mi ɛ ɣän ji̱n, /cä jɛ dee lat. | Amharic: እኔ አንተን ብሆን ኖሮ አልጠቅሰውም ነበር።
Example: If she were here, she would help us.
Nuer: Mi tëë wanɛ mɛ, derɛ kɔn luäk | Amharic: እሷ እዚህ ብትሆን ትረዳን ነበር። iswa izīh bitihon tiredani neberi
Example: If they knew the truth, they would be angry.
Nuer: Mi ci kɛn thuɔ̱k ŋa̱c, dee kɛ gaak . | Amharic: እውነቱን ቢያውቁ ይናደዱ ነበር። iwinetun bīyawik’u yinadedu neber

4. Third Conditional

The third conditional is used for hypothetical situations in the past that did not happen.

Kä kɔndi̱cinal in diɔ̱kdiɛn ɛ mi̱ cäät kɛ rɛy gua̱th ëë ci̱ wä min /kän tuɔɔk.

Formula: If + past perfect, + would have + past participle
Example: If I had worked harder, I might have passed the exam"
Nuer: Mi cä lät ɛ bum ɛlɔ̱ŋ ɛnë, dɔ̱ŋ dee ɣän ɣɔn bäl | Amharic: ጠንክሬ ብሰራ ኖሮ ፈተናውን ማለፍ እችል ነበር።
Example: If she had seen you, she would have loved you.
Nuer: Mi cɛ ji̱ nɛn ɛnë , derɛ ji̱ nhɔk . | Amharic: አንቺን ብታይ ኖሮ ትወድሽ ነበር።
Example: If they had invited us, we would have attended.
Nuer: Mi ci kɛn kɔn cɔl ɛnë, dënɛ wä guäth ɛmɔ. | Amharic: ጋብዘውን ቢሆን ኖሮ እንገኝ ነበር። gabizewin bīhon noro inigenyi neberi
